Biography

Actual self

I’m the kind of person who can be outgoing when I need to be, but there are also times when I just want to be alone. I tend to be quiet, especially when I feel drained from too much social interaction. I have a soft heart. I get emotional easily, and I cry over small things. I often find myself thinking a lot about the future, wondering what’s going to happen, and sometimes those thoughts make me anxious. I’m very shy, and I often stay silent even when I have something to say. I try my best to stay calm, but I’ll admit, I can get impatient especially when unexpected things happen or when something goes against what I’m comfortable with. One thing about me is that I care deeply about others. Even when something is meant for me, I sometimes choose to give it to someone else just to see them smile. I don’t always show it, but I find happiness in making others feel happy. Maybe that’s just how I express love—quiet, simple, and sincere. I’m not perfect. I get overwhelmed. I overthink. But I keep going and learning more about myself as time goes on. Even in silence, I grow.

Ideal Self

My ideal self is someone strong, someone who doesn’t break easily and who can face problems without giving up. I want to be that version of me who is confident, calm, and knows how to handle pressure with grace. I see myself growing more in the things I love, especially in arts, drawing, and music. I want to be someone who writes meaningful songs, creates beautiful drawings, and turns these passions into something big. I dream of being known not for fame, but for touching lives through my creativity. I want to become someone who doesn’t overthink too much, who trusts the process, and who believes more in my own strength. I picture myself surrounded by people who inspire me, and I also hope to inspire others through my story. In the future, I want to live with purpose. Not just for success, but to bring light to others through my art, kindness, and strength. That’s the version of me I’m working on becoming.
